Internship: Institute for Research in Technology (IIT)

10/2018 - 06/2019

Master's Thesis in collaboration with Institute for Research in Technology (IIT)

Thesis: "International Trade from the perspective of Game Theory"
This thesis provides, from the perspective of game theory, a qualitative and quantitative analysis of the trade conflict between the United States and China.
Project objectives
  • Analyze the different strategies that both countries can take through different variables, bearing in mind that cooperation is a key point to achieve a beneficial collective effect.
  • Assume possible strategic interactions between both economic powers in the international system from the perspective of game theory.
  • Carry out regressions with MATLAB to correlate macro-economic data from both countries.
  • Through the correlations made, build a tool to reach possibles solutions to economic and trade policy proposals between the United States and China.
